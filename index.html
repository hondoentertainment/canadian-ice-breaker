How to play

  1. Pick a question from the list below.
  2. Gather a small group of 3–5 people and read the question out loud.
  3. Give everyone a turn to answer. Listen, laugh, follow the tangents.
  4. When you're ready for a fresh mix, break into new groups and pick again.
